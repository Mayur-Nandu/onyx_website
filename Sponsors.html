WHY SPONSOR US ?

Help us to help you. Active co-operation is the path to mutual progress. We are a budding organisation and in constant need of resources to satiate our needs. We are well-versed with the Indian Aerospace Competitions, with over 40 years of cumulative experience, which gives us an upper hand in all walks of the tests. Having been ranked in the top tier has always been a testament of our progress and relentless dedication. Be it the design aspects or manufacturing techniques, our esteemed student-engineers always out perform their peer teams. We believe our only competition is our past self, which is why we strive to better ourselves every passing day. Team ONYX also organises various events in different colleges and hosts an Aermodelling competition called X in KJSCE every year. The event has a footfall of over 5000 people and is increasing in strength and value every passing year. This is guaranteed to attract larger crowds towards your organisation if you choose to partner with us. Please do check our gallery for more detailed insight to our projects.
