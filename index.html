Team Onyx

Aeromodeling team Of KJSCE



Team Onyx is a student-run Aero design club that was formed in 2011 and has been active ever since. We design and fabricate Remote-Controlled Model Aircrafts and test it’s ingenuity in various Aero design and Aeromodelling competitions. Our primary focus is competing in national and international stage in the SAE Aero design Competitions.

Media and Event

Autodesk: Autodesk Industries held an event at CIDCO exhibition centre Vashi in 2016 where we displayed our model.

Abhiyantriki: Abhiyantriki is K. J. Somaiya College’s annual technical fest which boasts participation from organizations like DRDO, ISRO, BARC and in 2017, Team Onyx showcased their model to a footfall of over 15000 people

MakerMela: MakerMela is India’s largest Maker movement. In their annual fest in 2018 at Somaiya Vidyavihar, Team Onyx again had its model up for display for thousands of people to see, observe and understand the processes behind making this plane.

Drone Competition: Apart from showcasing our works in various events, Team Onyx has also organized a drone racing competition in KJSCE for participants to test their piloting skills and win various cash prizes.

Workshops


TThe aeromodelling workshop organized by Team Onyx is one of the most anticipated events of the academic year. Usually a 2-Day workshop, students from various educational institutes across Mumbai participate to have a better understanding of concepts related to aviation and aerodynamics and apply the same concepts while making their own plane the following day taking into consideration various design elements and manufacturing processes.



This year witnessed a participation of about 100 aviation enthusiasts. Various aspects of aerodynamic concepts, manufacturing process, material selection, and design softwares were discussed in an interactive session. All these concepts were implemented while the students were given a kit to build their own P51 Mustang RC Aircraft.
